Potions can be crafted from other raw materials at cauldrons.

There is a percentage of a chance to fail in making an item and wasting the resources, a better alchemy skill lessens that chance.
Better items will have a much higher chance to fail.

On crafting an item, a splash sound and smoke should rise up from the cauldron.
On failing to craft an item, a dull sizzle sound will play without smoke.
